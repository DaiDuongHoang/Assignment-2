Australian Bushfires

An interactive dashboard exploring the impact and spread of bushfires across Australia.

Fire Hotspots Across Australia

During the Black Summer of 2019-2020, Australia experienced one of its most catastrophic bushfire seasons. This map reveals the sheer scale of the crisis, displaying satellite detections in January 2020.

The massive clusters along the southeast coast show the devastating intensity of the fires. Meanwhile, the Wheatbelt fires in Western Australia and savanna burns in the Northern Territory highlight the widespread, continent-spanning nature of the fire activity.
